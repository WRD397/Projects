Problem Statement : 

A non banking finance company. 

* Deals with individual where the RISK IS HIGH.
* Individuals who got rejected from the banks.
* As, the risk is high, the INTEREST RATE is also HIGHER than usual banks.

** So, the trade off is that if the individual returns the loan the profit is very high but the chance that the individual pay back loan is low.
** The problem statement is about telling whether the person will repay or not beforehand to maximize the profit.
